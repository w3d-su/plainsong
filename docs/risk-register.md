## Immediate risk burn-down order

1. Keep WYSIWYG Experimental/off by default; stable/default promotion remains blocked by the explicit promotion checkbox and requires a Decision Log entry.
2. Keep the opt-in actual IME (Zhuyin + Pinyin) harness and the pointer/selection/B13 gates runnable as regression coverage before any WYSIWYG promotion.
3. Keep partial folded-span copy/paste policy documented and covered by EditorKit tests.
4. Keep link visual folding and deferred constructs out of Experimental WYSIWYG until their own native gates pass.
5. Keep R17 as post-M5 polish; do not fold adjustable/native sidebar work into WYSIWYG gates.
6. Keep release hardening separate from Phase 2 feature work.
