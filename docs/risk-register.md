## Immediate risk burn-down order

1. Implement the user-facing WYSIWYG UX and mode-integration gates only after the closed R18 mechanism stays green: delimiter edge-snapping, the three-state `⌘⇧P` cycle, persisted layout migration, kill switch / deterministic recovery, and Experimental labeling.
2. Keep the opt-in actual IME (Zhuyin + Pinyin) harness and the pointer/selection/B13 gates runnable as regression coverage before any user-facing WYSIWYG mode.
3. Keep partial folded-span copy/paste policy documented and covered by EditorKit tests.
4. Keep user-facing WYSIWYG blocked until `docs/wysiwyg-release-checklist.md` §C/§D/§F are green.
5. Keep R17 as post-M5 polish; do not fold adjustable/native sidebar work into WYSIWYG gates.
6. Keep release hardening separate from Phase 2 feature work.
